***Stream and Buffer in node-js

1) what is buffer
-temporary storage spot for a chunk of data that is being transferred from one place to another
-The buffer is filled with data,then passed along
-Transfer small chunks of data at a time
-

2) Stream
-Can create stream in node-js to transfer data
-Increase performance.
- Streams are objects that let you read data from a source or write data to a destination in continuous fashion. 
In Node.js, there are types of streams − Readable − Stream which is used for read operation. 
Writable − Stream which is used for write operation.
